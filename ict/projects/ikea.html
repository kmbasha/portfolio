IKEA Multi-Location Integrated Technology

Yas Island Abu Dhabi, Dubai Festival City, Al Ain | $3.8M Program
IKEA Global Standards SIRA Compliant Hybrid Methodology EVM (CPI 1.04) Axxon VMS Matrox 3x6 8 operators

Project Overview

Led $3.8M integrated technology program across 3 IKEA locations (Yas Island Abu Dhabi, Dubai Festival City, Al Ain) managing 6 concurrent sub-projects: CCTV 500+ cameras, control room, VMS, POS integration, Qmatic CEM, digital signage. Coordinated 30+ team members and 8 vendor partners across 18-month delivery cycle.

Key Achievements

  • Delivered turnkey solution on-time and under budget (EVM: CPI 1.04, SPI 1.01)
  • Zero critical defects meeting IKEA global corporate standards
  • Reduced shrinkage by 35% (exceeded 30% target)
  • Full SIRA compliance (UAE Security Industry Regulatory Agency)
  • Successfully supported IKEA internal security audit

Program Management Approach

Hybrid Waterfall-Agile Methodology

  • Infrastructure delivery (CCTV, cabling, control room): Waterfall methodology due to physical construction dependencies
  • POS-CCTV integration software: Agile 2-week sprints for iterative development and testing
  • PMBOK-aligned with emphasis on integration management coordinating multiple concurrent sub-systems

Procurement & Contract Management

  • Developed RFP for security systems incorporating IKEA global security standards and UAE SIRA compliance
  • Evaluated 6 bids using scoring matrix: IKEA-approved vendor status (40%), technical capability (30%), commercial proposal (20%), local support (10%)
  • Negotiated turnkey fixed-price contract ($3.8M) with 12 milestone payments, 10% retention
  • Verified contractor compliance: SIRA installation license, IKEA corporate vendor approval, insurance, safety certifications

Earned Value Management

  • Tracked EVM for $3.8M program: CPI 1.04 (under budget), SPI 1.01 (on schedule)
  • Monthly variance analysis identified CCTV installation 5% ahead of schedule enabling early POS integration testing
