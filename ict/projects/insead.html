INSEAD Business School - Complete ELV Program

Amphitheatre, Training Rooms, Auditorium - Executive Education
Crestron Control AV Systems Video Conferencing Lecture Capture Executive Education

Project Overview

Client: INSEAD (French International Business School), Abu Dhabi Campus | Duration: 2015-2016 | Budget: $600K

Led complete ELV (Extra Low Voltage) program for amphitheatre, training rooms, and auditorium. Managed team of 6-8 resources including AV consultants, Crestron programmers, acoustic specialists.

Technical Scope Delivered

Amphitheatre Audio System

  • Line array speakers: Professional audio system for 200+ capacity
  • Delay speakers: Ensuring even sound distribution
  • Acoustic treatment: Wall and ceiling treatments for optimal sound
  • DSP audio processing: Automatic mixing and equalization
  • Assistive listening systems: Support for hearing-impaired attendees

Training Room AV (Multiple Rooms)

  • Interactive displays: 80" and 65" for training sessions
  • Wireless presentation: Barco ClickShare for easy screen sharing
  • Ceiling microphone arrays: Clear audio capture for remote participants
  • PTZ cameras: Auto-tracking instructor movements

Lecture Capture Systems

  • Automated recording: Capture lectures for on-demand viewing
  • Multi-camera switching: Instructor, presentation, audience views
  • Live streaming: Real-time broadcast to remote campuses
  • Content management: Video library with search and playback

Video Conferencing

  • Cisco Webex Room Kits: Professional VC endpoints
  • Distance learning: Connect Abu Dhabi campus with INSEAD France and Singapore
  • Multi-point calls: Simultaneous connections with multiple sites
  • Integration: Seamless connection to global INSEAD network

Crestron Control Systems

  • One-touch meeting start: Faculty press one button to start entire room
  • Automated lighting scenes: Presentation, video conference, break modes
  • Integrated AV control: Displays, audio, cameras, recording all centralized
  • Touch panel interfaces: Intuitive control for non-technical users
  • Preset configurations: Common scenarios pre-programmed

DSP Audio Processing

  • Automatic echo cancellation for video conferencing
  • Noise reduction for clearer audio
  • Automatic gain control
  • Audio routing and mixing

Stakeholder Coordination

  • INSEAD academic staff: Faculty input on teaching requirements
  • IT department: Network integration and ongoing support
  • Facilities team: Coordination on physical installations
  • AV consultants: System design and specifications
  • Crestron programmers: Custom control system programming

Quality Assurance

  • Acoustic testing: Professional measurements of audio performance
  • Video quality validation: Resolution, color accuracy, brightness
  • User acceptance: Faculty testing sessions before go-live
  • Load testing: Validate systems with full classrooms

Training & Change Management

  • Faculty training: How to use AV systems effectively
  • IT support training: Troubleshooting and maintenance
  • User guides: Quick reference cards at each room
  • Help desk procedures: Rapid response for technical issues

Outcomes & Benefits

  • Met international business school standards
  • Enhanced teaching/learning experience with modern technology
  • Flexible room configurations supporting various teaching styles
  • Reliable technology supporting executive education programs
  • Seamless integration with INSEAD global campuses
  • Professional environment meeting expectations of executive participants
