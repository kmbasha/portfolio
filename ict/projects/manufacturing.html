Manufacturing Infrastructure Programs

Zero Production Downtime Upgrades
Structured Cabling Cisco Catalyst HP Aruba Industrial WiFi Zero Downtime

Project Overview

Clients: Manufacturing (automotive parts, food processing, industrial equipment) | Duration: 2022-2024 | Budget: $80K-$200K per facility

Led network infrastructure upgrades for manufacturing facilities ensuring zero production downtime. Managed teams of 6-10 resources, coordinated with production managers for maintenance windows.

Technical Scope

Structured Cabling Upgrade

  • Cat6A data cabling: 1,000+ drops per facility for workstations, scanners, industrial devices
  • OM3/OM4 fiber backbone: High-speed connections between production areas
  • Industrial-grade connectors: Ruggedized for harsh environments
  • Testing/certification: Fluke DSX-5000 comprehensive validation

Network Core/Edge Upgrade

  • Cisco Catalyst core switches: Redundant pair for high availability
  • HP Aruba distribution/access: Scalable architecture
  • 10Gbps backbone: Supporting high-bandwidth applications
  • PoE+ deployment: For IP phones, cameras, wireless APs
  • VLAN segmentation: Production/office/guest/IoT networks
  • QoS configuration: Priority for production-critical traffic

Industrial WiFi

  • Industrial-grade APs: Warehouse and production floor
  • Mesh WiFi: Coverage in large open areas
  • Production floor connectivity: Handheld scanners, tablets, IoT sensors
  • 24/7 uptime requirement: Redundant controllers and failover

Implementation Strategy

  • Parallel installation: New infrastructure deployed alongside existing
  • Maintenance window coordination: Work scheduled during planned downtime
  • Cutover strategy: Quick switchover minimizing disruption
  • 24/7 on-call support: During transition period
  • Rollback procedures: Ready if issues encountered

Outcomes & Benefits

  • Zero manufacturing downtime during upgrades
  • 10Gbps backbone with full redundancy
  • 99.9%+ uptime post-implementation
  • Infrastructure supporting IoT and Industry 4.0 initiatives
  • Wireless connectivity for 200+ production floor devices
  • Scalable platform for future expansion
