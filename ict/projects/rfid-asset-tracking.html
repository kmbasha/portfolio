RFID Asset & Inventory Tracking

Airport & Manufacturing Real-Time Location Systems

Client Abu Dhabi Airport, ANABEEB (HOBAS) Manufacturing
Duration 2016-2018
Budget $400K-$600K
Role RFID Implementation Program Manager

Program Overview

Led enterprise RFID (Radio-Frequency Identification) implementation programs for comprehensive asset and inventory tracking across warehouse and manufacturing environments.

Technical Implementation

RFID Infrastructure Deployment

  • RFID gateway readers at entry/exit points and strategic locations
  • Fixed readers for warehouse zones and storage areas
  • Handheld RFID readers for stock-taking and verification
  • RFID tag encoding station and provisioning system
  • Antenna placement optimization through site surveys

Inventory Management Integration

  • Real-time location tracking system (RTLS) providing item visibility
  • Tracking across different stock locations and warehouse zones
  • Automated stock level monitoring with low-stock alerts
  • Location-based alerts for misplaced inventory
  • Bin-level accuracy for warehouse management

Finance Module Integration

  • Asset depreciation calculations automated based on asset lifecycle
  • Lending/checkout tracking workflow for equipment and tools
  • Preventive maintenance scheduling based on usage and time
  • Disposal workflow automation with approvals and documentation
  • Complete asset audit trail from procurement through disposal
  • Financial reporting integration for asset valuation
