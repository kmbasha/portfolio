Healthcare Infrastructure - 3 Major Institutions

Burjeel Hospital, Sheikh Khalifa Medical City, NMC Hospital
Healthcare IT HIPAA-Aligned Medical Device Security 24/7 Uptime Audit Support PMP

Project Overview

Healthcare IT infrastructure across 3 major institutions in UAE. Budget: $2M-$4M per facility. Implemented network segmentation and security controls for HIPAA-aligned data protection requirements.

Compliance & Security

Healthcare Data Protection

Network segmentation complying with HIPAA-aligned data protection requirements separating medical devices, clinical systems, administrative networks.

Medical Device Network Security

  • Implemented FDA-recommended controls for medical device networks
  • Isolated VLANs for medical devices
  • Restricted access to critical medical systems
  • Continuous monitoring of medical device network traffic

Access Controls

  • Role-based access controls for clinical systems
  • Audit logging for PHI (Protected Health Information) access
  • Encryption for data in transit and at rest
  • Multi-factor authentication for privileged access

Stakeholder Management

  • Complex stakeholder landscape: Clinical staff (physicians, nurses), IT department, biomedical engineering, facilities, administration, patients (indirect)
  • Change impact assessment identified nurse workflows, physician access patterns as critical adoption factors
  • Training tailored to clinical roles with emphasis on minimal disruption to patient care

Quality & Risk Management

  • Infection Control: Strict adherence to hospital infection control procedures during installations
  • Patient Safety Risk: Risk assessments for any work affecting clinical systems, clinical engineering sign-off required
  • Quality hold points included clinical system testing with nurse validation before go-live

Regulatory Compliance

  • Supported UAE Health Authority inspections
  • Civil Defense inspections for fire safety
  • Internal clinical audits
  • Provided network documentation and compliance evidence

Outcomes

  • Zero critical system failures during patient operations
  • Met healthcare data protection requirements
  • Successful Health Authority and Civil Defense inspections
  • 24/7 uptime for critical clinical systems (EMR, PACS, nurse call, telephony)
  • Network segmentation protecting medical devices
  • 95%+ client satisfaction, supported multiple clinical audits without findings
