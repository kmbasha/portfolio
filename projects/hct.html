Higher Colleges of Technology - 18 Campus Program

200+ Classrooms Over 4-Year Program
Program Management EVM (CPI 1.06) Gov Procurement 200+ Classrooms 4-Year Program MoE Standards

Project Overview

Transformed 200+ smart classrooms across 18 HCT campuses over 4-year program. Coordinated 25-30 resources across 18 locations. Standardized technology platform enabling distance learning connecting 18 campuses.

Key Achievements

  • Program CPI 1.06 (under budget through standardization)
  • 40% reduction in support complexity
  • One-touch meeting start reduced setup from 10 minutes to 30 seconds
  • 90% reduction in IT support calls
  • Enabled 500+ inter-campus lectures annually

Technical Scope

  • Smart Classrooms (200+ rooms): Crestron/Extron control, interactive whiteboards (SMART Board 75"-86", Promethean), wireless presentation (Barco ClickShare)
  • Distance Learning: Video conferencing per campus (Cisco/Polycom), centralized video bridge
  • Digital Signage: Campus-wide networks (Scala, Signagelive CMS)
