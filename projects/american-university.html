American University - Campus Infrastructure

Campus-wide Network for 5,000+ Students & Staff
Structured Cabling Fiber Backbone Aruba WiFi 5,000+ Users Campus Network

Project Overview

Client: American University, Abu Dhabi | Duration: 2017 | Budget: $400K

Led campus-wide cabling program for 5,000+ students and staff. Managed team of 8-10 resources including network consultants, cabling contractors, WiFi specialists.

Technical Scope

Structured Cabling

  • Cat6/Cat6A data cabling to all buildings
  • OM3/OM4 fiber backbone between buildings - 10Gbps capability
  • Redundant fiber paths for critical buildings
  • Fluke DSX-5000 certification

Wireless Network

  • Aruba controller and AP deployment
  • High-density WiFi for lecture halls/libraries
  • Outdoor WiFi for campus grounds
  • VLAN segmentation: students/staff/guests/admin

Network Core

  • 10Gbps backbone with redundant core switches
  • PoE+ for APs and VoIP phones
  • QoS for video conferencing
  • Support for 5,000+ concurrent devices

Outcomes

  • 99.5% WiFi coverage across campus
  • Zero network downtime during semester
  • BYOD support for 10,000+ devices
  • Scalable infrastructure for future growth
