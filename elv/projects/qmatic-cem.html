Qmatic Customer Experience Management

Multi-Sector Deployment across Government, Healthcare & Education

Client Ministry of Human Resources (MOHRE), British Council, Healthcare Facilities
Duration 2015-2020
Budget $2M+ across deployments
Role Customer Experience Program Manager

Program Overview

Led enterprise-wide Customer Experience Management programs implementing Qmatic queue management systems across healthcare, government, and education sectors. Managed deployments ranging from single-location to multi-department implementations spanning different geographical locations.

Key Implementations

MOHRE (50+ Government Centers)

  • Deployed centralized queue management system across 50+ Ministry of Human Resources centers nationwide
  • Integrated Happiness Meter with government CX platforms for real-time citizen satisfaction feedback
  • Developed custom kiosks for service ticket dispensing
  • Implemented digital signage network for service information and wait time displays
  • Integrated with POS systems and payment gateways for cashier services
  • Established centralized management platform for queue analytics and reporting across all centers

British Council IELTS Section

  • Implemented integrated queue system with digital signage for IELTS testing and training center
  • Managed customer flow optimization reducing wait times by 40%
  • Deployed appointment integration with testing schedules

Healthcare Facilities

  • Deployed queue management for multiple clinics and hospitals
  • Integrated with patient management systems
  • Implemented priority queue for emergency cases
